Hello there!

My name is Eduardo Henke, I’m from Brazil(from this pretty island) and I’m currently a co-founder/partner/backend-engineer at Voltbras.

I’m finishing my B.Sc. in Computer Science at Federal University of Santa Catarina, and I’m currently applying to PhD programmes in Computer Science, regarding my research interests.

My principle

My principle(as someone explained the term) is to:

Simplify development through the improvement of programming languages

To achieve that, I believe that we need to focus on:

  • easier ways to verify correctness
  • easier ways to model a problem
  • faster software

I strongly believe that type systems(and other kinds of static analysis) are the way to achieve that.

Research interests

Primary research interests(with some degree of knowledge and actively learning):

  • type theory
  • dependent types
  • cubical types
  • homotopy type theory
  • category theory
  • gradual typing

Secondary research interests(basic knowledge):

  • high-level optimization(e.g. grin)
  • program synthesis
  • design by contract
  • constraint-based programming
  • aspect-oriented programming
  • feature-oriented programming

Projects

  • TaPL impl: implementations of the type systems’ from TaPL, in Haskell
  • dep-tt: a dependent type system, in Haskell
  • ts-ocpp: fully-typed and functionally written implementation of OCPP(Open Charge Point Protocol, i.e. protocol to communicate with Electric Vehicle chargers), in Typescript
  • simple lang: a simple compiler for a simple language, in Rust
